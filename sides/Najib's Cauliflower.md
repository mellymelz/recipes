# Najib's Cauliflower

like the dish from Nuba

## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 small head cauliflower, in florets
- olive oil
- salt and pepper
- 1 tsp cumin
- sumac
- 1/2 a lemon of juice + zest too

## Method

- Heat oven to 400F 
- Mix together and roast 45 minutes.
- Turn/move every 15 minutes.

### Notes 

great with a tahini sauce!
