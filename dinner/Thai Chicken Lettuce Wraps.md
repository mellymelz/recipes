# Thai Chicken Lettuce Wraps

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground chicken/chopped chicken thighs or breasts
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 2 Tbsp red curry paste
- 1 Tbsp ginger, minced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 4 green onion, chopped
- 1 cup shredded cabbage or slaw mix
- 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
- 1/2 head iceburg lettuce 
- 5 thai basil leaves, chiffonade

## Method

- Heat oil in a pan then add chicken to cook.
- Then add curry paste, ginger, garlic, pepper and slaw then cook 3 minutes.
- Then add hoisin and green onion stir to combine then remove from heat.
- Add basil.
- Wrap in lettuce and chow down

### Notes

It's more delicate but i love butter lettuce for this as well
