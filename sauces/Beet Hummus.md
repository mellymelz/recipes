# Beet Hummus

sweet beets

## Ingredients

- 2 beets, cooked, cooled, peeled and chopped
- 1 15oz can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 3 cloves of garlic
- 2 tsp lemon zest
- 3 Tbsp lemon juice
- 1/4 cup tahini
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp cumin

## Method

Mix all in a food processor until creamy
