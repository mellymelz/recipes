# Garlic Butter

#### This is straight out of the Milestones recipe binders muahaha

## Ingredients 

* 2 lbs butter, soft
* 1oz garlic, minced
* 1oz garlic, roasted
* 1.5oz coarse salt
* 2 floz lemon juice
* 1 floz hot sauce
* 1 floz parsley

## Method

Mix all that goodness and you got the garlic butter you wanna spread on all your grilled meats!
