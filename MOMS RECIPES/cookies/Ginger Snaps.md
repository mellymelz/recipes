# Ginger Snaps

#### Yield: 7 dozen

## Ingredients
- 1 cup shortening
- 2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 2 large eggs
- 4 cups all purpose flour
- 4 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 2 Tbsp ground ginger

## Method
- Blend together
- Shape into 1 inch balls
- Roll in sugar - DO NOT FLATTEN
- Bake at 350F for 15 minutes
