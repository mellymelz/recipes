# Double Chocolate Cookies

Makes a nice soft cookie, can be made minty if you are a choco-mint fiend like myself.

## Ingredients

- 1.75 cup all purpose flour
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- 1/3 cup cocoa powder
- 1 cup chocolate chips 
- 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 egg
- 2 Tbsp milk
- 1 cup pecans or walnuts *optional
- To make it minty omit nuts and add 1 tsp mint extract

## Method

- Combine flour and soda, set aside.
- Cream butter and sugar, beat until fluffy.
- Beat in egg, then at low speed add cocoa and milk. (and mint if you are adding)
- Stir in dry ingredients, chocolate chips. (and nuts if you adding)
- Drop on cookie sheet with parchment and bake 350F for 12-13 minutes.
