# Basic Biscuits

makes 12

## Ingredients

* 1.75 cups all purpose flour
* 2.5 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp course salt
* 6 Tbsp cold unsalted butter, cubed
* 1/2 cup milk

## Method

Preheat oven to 450F 
Whisk together dry ingredients.
Cut butter in. 
Stir in milk.
Knead dough gently. 
Roll out and cut into biscuits.
Bake 13-15 minutes or until golden.
